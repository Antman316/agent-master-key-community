# Community Code of Conduct

We want Agent Master Key discussions to be useful, candid, and safe.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and address ideas and evidence rather than people.
- Share reproducible steps and redacted evidence when reporting a problem.
- Respect privacy, confidentiality, and coordinated security disclosure.
- Accept moderator requests to move account-specific or sensitive troubleshooting to a
  private support channel.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, doxxing, impersonation, or spam.
- Posting credentials, personal data, private logs, or another person's confidential data.
- Publishing exploit details that could put customers at risk before a coordinated fix.
- Deliberately misleading claims or repeated disruption of support discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content and restrict participation when needed
to protect people, credentials, or the community. Report conduct concerns privately to
`support@agentmasterkey.com`. Do not include credentials or unrelated personal information.
